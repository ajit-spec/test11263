India is the seventh-largest country by area and the most populous democracy in the world.
It is a land of diverse cultures, languages, and traditions.
With 28 states and 8 union territories, India is known for its rich heritage, ancient civilization, and vibrant festivals.
The country is also home to the world's highest peak, Mount Everest, and the longest river, the Ganges.
